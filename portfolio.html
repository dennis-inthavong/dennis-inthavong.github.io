Who I Am

I'm a first-year IT Systems Administration student at NAIT in Edmonton, combining formal education with hands-on lab work and two Google professional certifications. I'm actively building the skills needed to contribute immediately in both support and security contexts.

I've always been the kind of person who needs to know how things work — not just that they work. That curiosity is what pulled me into IT in the first place, and it's what keeps me reading articles after school hours and genuinely enjoying the process of figuring things out. For me, technology has never been just a career path — it's been a fascination since I first got my hands on a computer.

On the technical side, I'm currently building a home lab running pfSense to practice threat detection and incident response in a virtual environment while trying to build a long-term career goal in blue team SOC operations.
